About me

My name is Andrea Signori, and I am from Italy. I’m a Data Scientist and Analyst. I completed my Bachelor's degree in Economics and Finance at the University of Brescia in Italy in 2019, and I obtained my Master's degree in Data Science within a Double Degree program with Trier University (Germany) in 2022.

I developed my master thesis through a joint research collaboration with Harvard University, studying time-dependent demand forecasting methodologies across econometrics and machine learning. In 2025, I completed a PhD in Technology, Innovation, and Management, focusing on Data Science applications in the aviation industry.

Within my role, I aim to unveil the stories hidden in data—transforming raw numbers into insights that guide decisions, uncover trends, and bring clarity to complexity. My work spans a diverse range of domains rather than a single specialized niche. I have led and contributed to projects in aviation demand forecasting, economic time-series analysis, and sport performance and injury prevention within the healthcare sector.

This heterogeneity has strengthened my ability to quickly understand new contexts, ask the right questions, and adapt analytical frameworks across industries. I enjoy crafting compelling data narratives, whether through interactive dashboards, visualizations, or analytical reports, turning data into knowledge that empowers action and sparks understanding.
